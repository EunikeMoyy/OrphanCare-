Join our team

The transformation from institutions looking after children to a family and community based care system requires driven,
committed people – innovative thinkers, good planners, team players and effective doers – to steer the way forward.
With urbanisation and the rise in numbers of nuclear families there will be adjustments in the way we look after our children.
OrphanCare’s pioneering effort could set off a chain reaction in job opportunities within this new ‘paradigm’.
Our mission is to help every child in Malaysia grow up in a loving family environment.
If you feel as passionate about this as we do, consider a career with us.
Our growing team is energetic and enthusiastic about developing solutions to improve the lives of Malaysia’s less privileged children.
Our work environment is demanding but if you have empathy OrphanCare Foundation is a good place to begin a fulfilling career.
Meet our team

Head of Social Worker

Fadilah joined OrphanCare in January 2018 as Head of Social Worker.
She has a Bachelor’s Degree in Social Work from Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM) and Masters in Social Work from Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM).
She had 8 years experience of managing cases such as refugees, underprivileged, disabled children, orphans, child abuses & street children.
She also had experiences of handling court cases involving children.
Based on OrphanCare’s work core, is to help children be assimilated into families,
She used her experience working at a children’s institution to make her understand more on child needs & the harms.
She describes her work as meaningful, exciting and colorful.
